Genius inventor and billionaire playboy Tony Stark dons a technologically advanced suit of armor to become the superhero Iron Man. After a life-altering experience, Stark uses his technical expertise to fight evil and defend the world with repulsor rays, booster jets, and other high-tech weaponry built into his iconic suit. Originally a weapons manufacturer, Stark undergoes a moral shift and vows to use his inventions for good. He co-founds the Avengers alongside other iconic heroes like Thor and Hulk. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Robert Downey Jr. famously portrays the complex and charismatic character.
